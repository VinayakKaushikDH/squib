About riggability

Every candidate has at least these animation hooks:

  • Head/body bob (breathing)
  • Eye blink (scaleY)
  • Two independently-moving limbs or props

Limbless creatures (11, 12, 13, 14) use detached floating hands — the Rayman trick — so they can still type, wave, hold tools.

Your call → give me one number (or a few) and I'll build 4–5 full-fidelity variants with all 6 animation states (idle / thinking / working / sleeping / error / attention). Or describe a mix: "11's body, 06's screen face, 29's tentacle-hands" works.
